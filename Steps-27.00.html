Creating the Scatter Plot Chart 27


Steps:
  1. Click on an empty space on the canvas.
  2. From the Visualization pane Select Scatter Chart.
  3. Fill in the value fields provided below for the necessary fields. (State, YOY Births %, Total US Births, Births by Year)
