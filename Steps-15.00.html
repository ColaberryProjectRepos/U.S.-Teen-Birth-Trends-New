Creating Refresh Date Table 15



Steps:

  1. Click on Get Data, Click on Blank Query, write the formula for Refresh Date in the formula bar. = DateTime.LocalNow()
  2. Click on Convert to Table.
  3. Rename the Column name to “Refresh Date.”
  4. Rename the Query name to “Refresh Date.”
  5. Click on the Close and Apply button to save.
