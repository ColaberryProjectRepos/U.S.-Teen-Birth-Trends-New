Importing Data to Power BI 9


Steps

1. First, download the dataset and save it on your PC. There is only 1 table in the Folder known as “US & State Trends Teen Births”.
2. Open Power BI Desktop.
3. Click on “Get Data” tab.
4. Select Excel Workbook File and Select and Open the data set from where you saved it on your PC.
5. A preview of the data will be displayed, Click on Transform Data.
